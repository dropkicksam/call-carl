Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

Call Carl ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SMS-based home maintenance reminder service.

Quick Summary: We collect your phone number, home details, and maintenance preferences to send you text message reminders. We don't sell your data. You can opt out anytime by texting STOP.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you sign up for Call Carl, we collect:

  • Contact Information: Phone number, name (optional), email address (if provided)
  • Home Information: Address, year built, HVAC details, water heater information, and other home characteristics you share during onboarding
  • Maintenance Preferences: Your reminder preferences (comprehensive vs. essentials), custom tasks you create
  • Usage Data: Your responses to our messages (DONE, SKIP, HELP, etc.), task completion history

Information Collected Automatically

  • SMS Metadata: Message delivery status, timestamps, interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Mobile carrier information (provided by our SMS service provider, Twilio)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Our Service: Send timely SMS reminders for home maintenance tasks based on your home's specific needs
  • Personalize Your Experience: Tailor reminder schedules and task recommendations to your home and preferences
  • Improve Our Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our reminder logic and add relevant maintenance tasks
  • Communicate With You: Respond to your questions, send service updates, and provide customer support
  • Prevent Annoyance: Track engagement to avoid sending reminders to inactive users
  • Comply With Legal Obligations: Meet regulatory requirements for SMS messaging and data protection

3. SMS Messaging Terms

Consent to Receive Text Messages

By providing your phone number and texting START to Call Carl, you expressly consent to receive recurring automated text messages from us at the phone number provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your home's maintenance schedule. You may receive 2-8 messages per month depending on your reminder preferences.

Message & Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

Supported Carriers: Our service works with all major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Texting STOP to our service number
  • Texting UNSUBSCRIBE
  • Emailing us at hello@call-carl.com with your phone number and "unsubscribe" request

After you send STOP, we will send you one final message to confirm your unsubscription.

Getting Help

For help with our service, text HELP to our number or email hello@call-carl.com.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We only share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate Call Carl, as described below.

Service Providers

We use trusted third-party service providers to help us operate our service:

  • Twilio: SMS messaging platform that sends and receives text messages on our behalf
  • Supabase: Database hosting service that securely stores your information
  • Anthropic: AI service provider that helps parse your natural language responses

These providers only access your information as necessary to perform services on our behalf and are obligated to protect your data.

Household Members

If you add a partner or household member to your account, we will share relevant task information with them (e.g., when tasks are completed).

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data (need-to-know basis)

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: We keep your data while you're using Call Carl
  • Inactive Accounts: If you stop responding to messages, we mark your account as dormant but retain your data for 12 months in case you want to reactivate
  • After Unsubscribing: We retain your phone number for up to 12 months to honor your opt-out preference and prevent accidental re-subscription
  • Task History: We may retain aggregated, anonymized task completion data indefinitely for product improvement

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing hello@call-carl.com.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Access & Correction

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, and you can ask us to correct inaccurate information.

Deletion

You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions (e.g., we may need to retain some data for legal compliance).

Opt-Out

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by texting STOP.

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@call-carl.com with your request and phone number. We will respond within 30 days.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell your information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@call-carl.com or call/text our service number.

9. Children's Privacy

Call Carl is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@call-carl.com.

If you are accessing our service from outside the U.S., please be aware that your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.
